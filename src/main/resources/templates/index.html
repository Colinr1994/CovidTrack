Attention


Please review the following criteria either before or upon arrival at work:

- Was your temperature over 100 degrees?

- Have you been in close direct contact (*) with anyone who has tested positive for COVID-19 within the last 14 days?

- Are you experiencing any symptons associated with COVID-19 today or in the past 24 hours?

   - Fever or chills
   - A new cough, nasal congestion or runny nose
   - Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
   - Fatigue
   - Muscle or body aches
   - A headache that is not normal for you
   - New loss of taste or smell
   - Sore throat
   - Nausea or vomiting
   - Diarrhea

If the answer to any of the questions was "yes", please inform HR. Enter your email and Accept below to certify that you are NOT experiencing any of the symtoms listed above.
